70. 그룹별 조건에 맞는 식당 목록 출력하기

https://school.programmers.co.kr/learn/courses/30/lessons/131124

SELECT
    P.MEMBER_NAME,
    R.REVIEW_TEXT,
    DATE_FORMAT(REVIEW_DATE, '%Y-%m-%d') REVIEW_DATE
FROM MEMBER_PROFILE P INNER JOIN REST_REVIEW R ON P.MEMBER_ID=R.MEMBER_ID
WHERE P.MEMBER_ID=(
    SELECT
        MEMBER_ID
    FROM REST_REVIEW
    GROUP BY 1
    ORDER BY COUNT(1) DESC
    LIMIT 1)
ORDER BY 3, 2
